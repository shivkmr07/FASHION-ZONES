Return Policy

Last updated: January 2024

7 Days Return Policy

At Fashion Zones, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery of your order.

⚠️ Unboxing Video is Mandatory

Returns will only be accepted if the customer has a clear and complete unboxing video recorded at the time of receiving and opening the product package.

  • The unboxing video must show the sealed package before opening.
  • The video must clearly show the product condition upon delivery.
  • Without a valid unboxing video, return requests cannot be processed under any circumstances.
  • The video must be unedited and recorded in one continuous shot.

Return Eligibility Conditions

  • Return request must be raised within 7 days of the delivery date.
  • The product must be unused and in its original condition.
  • Product must be returned in its original packaging with all accessories and tags.
  • Products that are damaged due to misuse, negligence, or improper handling are not eligible for return.
  • Consumable or perishable items once opened are not eligible for return.

Eligible Return Reasons

  • Product received is damaged or defective.
  • Wrong product delivered (different from what was ordered).
  • Product is missing parts or accessories as described.
  • Product is significantly different from the description on our website.

Return Process

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery at shivkmr603@gmail.com or call 9546700242.
  • Share your order details, reason for return, and your unboxing video.
  • Our team will review your request within 2–3 business days.
  • If approved, we will arrange a pickup or provide return shipping instructions.
  • Refund or replacement will be processed within 7–10 business days after receiving the returned product.

Refund Method

Refunds will be processed to your original payment method or as store credit, based on your preference and the nature of the issue.
